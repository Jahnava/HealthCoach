Jahnava Alexa, LMT, CHHC is a Certified Holistic Health Coach and Food Blogger with a passion to help people eat healthy and live a healthy lifestyle. Most days you can find her in the kitchen creating new recipes or at the gym getting her move on! In a world flooded with diet information, Jahnava’s straight-forward content combine’s nutrition, fitness, healthy recipes, and DIY remedies. Best known for her fun and accessible approach to food & healthy living, she’s built her online wellness business from scratch using her phone and laptop. Jahnava works with people who are looking for ways to implement simple changes to feeling like their best and happiest selves.
